Amy & Leo

Watercolours at Sopley Mill

Amy & Leo were an absolute pleasure to work with. Their enthusiasm and passion made it so easy to take their ideas and run with them.

From their hanging-disk save the dates, to their amazing flower garland table plan the stationery acted as an anchor for the rest of their day, not to mention their beautiful Order of the Day booklet.

Being one of the first suppliers Amy & Leo commissioned we were more than happy to liaise with other suppliers during our development time, working closely with the wedding coordinator at Sopley Mill and their florist to make sure the theme worked seamlessly throughout the day. The florist took inspiration from the gorgeous floral illustration managing to match the colours and design perfectly!

The attention to detail was striking and the whole day was full of personal and fun details.

Freya created the most beautiful stationery for our special day, and in turn this set the style and theme for everything else. Everyone commented on how amazing it was, and we were very impressed how Freya got our ideas and styling that we had in mind straight away. Thank you!

Amy & Leo Glendon-Smith
