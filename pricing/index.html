- So What Does it Cost? -

Being in the wedding planning game ourselves we understand that although your heart might want one thing your bank account often says another, thats why we have tried our best to make our costs as black & white as we can so you can budget some fantastic - one of a kind- stationery into your wedding spreadsheet!

Due to the nature of our bespoke service we have a 2 part costing system which works pretty well:

 

PART 1 - DESIGN FEE

We charge a set design fee per item which covers our design time & skill.

This includes an initial meeting either in London or an hour’s drive from our studio. These fee’s are fixed no matter how wacky your ideas are.
 

PART 2 - MATERIALS & PRINTING

In addition to the design fee we charge for materials, printing and finishing seperately. This means we can play around with details and finishing touches to help keep your design within budget.

 

The guideline costs below are based on our real weddings but during each project we work with our couples to supply exact costs and discuss aspiration v’s cost where suitable along the way.

Finishing processes such as foiling, letterpress, embossing, thermography & calligraphy will all carry an additional cost and will be discussed on an individual project basis.

Please use the guide below to pick & mix which items you might like us to work on, or if you would like a bespoke proposal please get in touch via our contact form and we will get back to you asap.
